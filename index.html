🌿Lets grow your Garden

This project helps you choose the best plants for your garden based on the size of your garden area.

WHY IT MATTERS:

🌍Environmental benefits:

  • Reduces carbon footprint: Growing food locally eliminates the emissions associated with transporting produce long distances
  • Decreases chemical use: You can choose to use organic and sustainable practices, which reduces the need for harmful pesticides and fertilizers.
  • Promotes biodiversity: Gardens can create habitats for beneficial insects like pollinators.

    • 💚Health wellbeing benefits

  • Improves nutrition: Access to fresh, homegrown produce encourages a diet richer in fruits and vegetables.
  • Provides physical activity: Gardening is a form of exercise that can help you stay fit and active
  • Reduces stress: The act of gardening can be a therapeutic hobby that lowers stress and provides a sense of accomplishment. Enhances taste: Homegrown food often tastes better because it is harvested at its peak freshness.

    • 💰Financial benefits

  • Saves money: Growing your own food can be a more affordable way to get fresh produce.
  • increases food security: Gardening provides a direct and reliable source of food, which is especially important in areas with food insecurity.
  • Builds community: Community gardens, in particular, bring people together to work towards a common goal.
  • Offers educational opportunities: Gardening is a great way to learn about where food comes from, and it can be used to teach children about nature and healthy eating.

Case Studies:

  • Urban Garden: Small space, high yield herbs.
  • Rural Garden: Larger plots supporting fruit trees.
