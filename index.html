‚ö† Challenges in Food Gardening

  1. Pests and Diseases:
    • Insects like aphids, beetles, and caterpillars can damage crops.
    • Fungal, bacterial, and viral diseases reduce yield.
    • Without proper control, crops may be lost.
  2. Climate & Weather Conditions:
    • Too much or too little rainfall affects growth.
    • Extreme temperatures and drought harm plants.
  3. Market & Storage Problems:
    • Difficulty selling produce without market access.
    • Perishable produce may go to waste without storage.
  4. Social & Cultural Barriers:
    • Communities may undervalue gardening.
    • Gender roles may limit participation.
